  • ESP32.

    The ESP32 is a powerful microcontroller known for its dual-core processing, integrated Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth features, making it a popular choice for diverse IoT projects to work with sensors.

  • MQ-7.

    The MQ7 sensor specializes in detecting carbon monoxide levels, providing accurate monitoring of indoor air quality, and is often utilized in conjunction with microcontrollers like the ESP32.

  • PIR.

    A proximity IR sensor, detects human presence by sensing changes in infrared radiation, crucial for triggering alerts in proximity-based applications, integrated with devices such as the ESP32.
